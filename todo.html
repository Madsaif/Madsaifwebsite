About This App

Todo App is a beautifully designed task management application that helps you stay organized, focused, and productive. Whether you're a student juggling assignments, a professional managing projects, or simply someone who wants to keep track of daily chores — this app makes it effortless.

With priority levels, and a clean user interface, Todo App ensures you never miss a deadline again. Plan your day, set goals, and track your progress with satisfaction.

Key Features

  • Create tasks with due dates and priorities
  • Categorize tasks into custom lists
  • Recurring task support (daily, weekly, monthly)
  • Progress tracking with completion stats
  • Dark mode for comfortable night use
  • Simple, clean, and offline experience
