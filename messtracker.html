About This App

MessTracker is a comprehensive mess management application designed specifically for students. It simplifies the complex task of tracking daily meals, calculating expenses, and managing mess accounts with ease and accuracy.

With an intuitive interface and powerful features, MessTracker helps you stay on top of your mess budget, avoid disputes over billing, and ensure everyone pays their fair share. Whether you're managing a hostel mess or a small shared kitchen, this app is your perfect companion.

Key Features

  • Daily meal tracking with easy check-in/check-out
  • Automatic expense calculation
  • Monthly and weekly summary reports
  • Offline mode support
